# FCAJ Community Day Report

### 1. General Event Information
- **Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day (First Cloud AI Journey Community Day)
- **Date & Time:** 09:00 - 12:00, Saturday, May 23, 2026
- **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Financial Tower, 2 Hai Trieu Street, Ben Nghe Ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City
- **Role:** Attendee

### 2. Speakers & Session Overview

#### Session 1: Context Is Everything: Making AI Actually Work for You
* **Speaker:** **Tinh Truong** - Platform Engineer at GoTymeX
* **Highlights:**
  * Why AI fails without context & what "context" really means.
  * From prompts to memory: how AI is evolving (Second AI Brain concept).
  * How better context leads to better results (practical mindset & tips).
  * Career insights & how students can start building with AI + Q&A.

#### Session 2: Friendly AI Assistant with Amazon Quick
* **Speaker:** **Anh Pham** - Cloud Consultant at G-AsiaPacific Vietnam
* **Highlights:**
  * **Quick Chat Agent**: AI assistants for exploring data, analyzing insights.
  * **Quick Flows**: Create intelligent workflows with natural language — no coding required.
  * **Quick Spaces**: Shared collaborative spaces that turn individual insights into team knowledge.
  * **Quick Sight**: Build dashboards and reports from raw data using natural language.

#### Session 3: From Edge To Origin: CloudFront as Your Foundation
* **Speaker:** **Thinh Nguyen** - DevOps Engineer at FCAJ
* **Highlights:**
  * Amazon CloudFront for every workload.
  * Cost optimization with Amazon CloudFront.
  * Security capabilities.
  * Enhanced reliability with Amazon CloudFront.
  * Enhanced performance with Amazon CloudFront.

#### Session 4: 36 hrs with LotusHacks – Building UTMorpho from Idea to Reality
* **Speaker:** **Team VIB** (Thao Nguyen, Mai Nguyen, Uyen Le - GenAI Engineers at VIB)
* **Highlights:**
  * Why we joined LotusHacks.
  * From zero to idea – The brainstorming journey.
  * Defining the problem & shaping UTMorpho.
  * Building under pressure – 36-hour development sprint.

#### Session 5: Non-Determinism of "Deterministic" LLM Settings
* **Speaker:** **Duc Dao** - Solutions Architect at Cloud Kinetics

  * Practical impacts.
  * Mitigation strategies.

#### Session 6: Enterprise-Grade Multi-Agent System: The Case of Startup Credit Scoring
* **Speaker:** **Vy Lam** - Senior Business Systems Analyst at VPBank
* **Highlights:**

  * Blueprint of a Virtual Credit Committee.

### 3. Key Takeaways & Gained Skills
* **Multi-Agent Design Architecture:** Learned how to structure complex tasks into modular agents that collaborate, improving LLM accuracy and workflow efficiency.
* **Content Delivery Networks (CDNs):** Gained practical insights on setting up Amazon CloudFront for static and dynamic resources, directly applicable to optimizing page speed for the Web IT Coach project.
* **LLM Internal Mechanics:** Understood the underlying probability distribution of tokens and how hardware inference configurations affect response repeatability.
* **Industry Networking:** Connected directly with practicing software developers, platform engineers, and architects from leading institutions like VPBank, VIB, GoTymeX, and Cloud Kinetics.
